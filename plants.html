The English Ivy also know as Common Ivy is a popular outdoor plant. It's very carefree, sprawling around trees and even covering full walls. Ivy can actually climb into the canopy of younge trees and cause them to fall over! Despite it's rebellious nature, the Ivy has some great benefits as an indoor plant (Just don't let take over!). The ivy is a great air purifier and also helps recude mold!

The Chinese Evergreen can be found in tropical forests, growing on shaded floors in Asia. Although it's named "evergreen", it can be found with other colours such as red and yellow.

The spider plant is a faster grower and is great for removing toxins. It can remove up to 90 percent withing two days. NASA also recommends it to people with dust allergies.

The Aloe Vera is a very easy to maintain plant.Named as one of the best air purifier plants, the Aloe Vera releases oxygen throughout the night, perfect for a bedroom.
